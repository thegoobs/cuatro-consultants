Complete preliminary planning, street, drainage and water distribution system for this rural hill country subdivision.  This subdivision consists of 185 lots averaging 1.25 acres in size, underground electric and telecommunications and a multi-plate arch bridge crossing Yorks Creek.   The bridge crossing includes 4 – 21’ 0” wide x 8’11” tall steel arches to pass the 100-year storm.   
* 17,300 LF of Street and Culvert Crossings
* 16,230 LF of 2”, 3”, and 4” PVC of Water Main
* Underground Electrical Improvements
